Welcome to Healthy INDIA,Safe INDIA

As we all know that the covid-19 pandemic has affected all the people globally both directly & indirectly, which has affected the mental & social health of person & frontline workers who are working day & night without a day off. There is a lot of pessimism that has developed from the lockdowns, working in stressful conditions, temporary unemployment, isolation from family, home schooling of children, no interaction with friends and colleagues & unable to celebrate joyful events, which has ultimately affected the mental & social health of public adversely.

To help the people who are greatly affected by this ongoing pandemic, we have come up to an idea as to how to help people whose mental and social health is impacted. We have developed a site which would allow us to connect to the recovered covid-19 patients and doctors and other health care workers around the Indian subcontinent to give them a platform where they all can share their experiences in the pandemic time with the whole community.

This site will connect people who have suffered covid-19 to the psychologists, counsellors and doctors from anywhere in India to help them recover from depression or stress. By this site a proper platform would be given to the people to express their experiences and feeling in these times. Various chat rooms or groups will be made for discussion & consultation.
